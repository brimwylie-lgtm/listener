How to use
For replays (webinar/podcast):
Open the video in another Chrome tab. Select "Tab audio," click Start, then pick that tab in the Chrome share dialog. Check "Share tab audio."

Playback speed: Works at any speed — 1x, 1.5x, 2x. The audio signal is what gets transcribed.

When done: Hit Copy and paste into Claude. Include a note like "Speaker 1 is the host, Speaker 2 is the guest" if you know who's who.

Chrome or Edge only — Firefox does not support Web Speech API.
