Guide
Recording a webinar or podcast
1
Open the video in a separate Chrome tab — don't play it yet
2
Come back here, set Audio source to Tab
3
Hit Start recording — Chrome will ask which tab to share
4
Select your video tab and tick "Share tab audio" — this is easy to miss
5
Go to the video tab and hit play. Any playback speed works.
6
When done, come back and click Stop recording
7
Hit Transcribe on the recording — takes about 10–30 seconds
8
Hit Copy and paste into Claude for analysis
Session setup tips
Set speakers before transcribing. Enter names in the left panel — they appear on every speaker badge automatically.
Click any speaker badge in the transcript to rename it on the fly if the diarization got it wrong.
Description field — paste the video description or episode summary. It gets included at the top of the exported transcript so Claude has full context.
Groq API key
Free key at console.groq.com — no credit card needed. Free tier allows ~7 hours of transcription per day. Your key is saved in your browser only, never sent anywhere except Groq.
File formats
Recordings save as .webm (Chrome) or .ogg (Firefox). Both work with Groq directly. If you need an MP3, use a free converter like cloudconvert.com.
